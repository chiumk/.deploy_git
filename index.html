Research Topics

My research interests are algorithms, graph theory and computational geometry. In the ERATO Kawarabayashi Large Graph Project, I work on how to digitalize objects from the Euclidean space to the digital space such that they have similar properties as the Euclidean geometry. Moreover, I also work some other geometric problems.
